Monster Builder

This wizard will step through the creation of Starfinder NPCs and monsters.

Heads up! Special abilities are not yet fully integrated into the builder. Currently any special abilities you choose will be printed below the stat block but any additions to the stat block (e.g. special attacks or senses) will need to be added manually.

Additionally not all template grafts have been added yet. Only simple/dragon grafts are available.
